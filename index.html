REF — AI READINESS DIAGNOSTIC

How ready is this environment for AI?

A structured read on stakeholder, process, data, and governance readiness — plus a separate ecosystem score — scored against the scale of the initiative you're actually planning.

Stakeholder 01

The people who'll use this day-to-day were involved in defining what it should do.

Leadership and the front line agree on what success looks like, and by when.

The team has the skills, or a training plan, to work alongside this tool once it's live.

Process 02

Core workflows are documented well enough that someone new could follow them without asking a specific person.

These workflows move without redundant steps, duplicate data entry, or unnecessary manual handoffs.

Process owners regularly review and update how work actually gets done, rather than running on outdated habit.

Data 03

Critical data lives in accessible, documented systems — not scattered across spreadsheets or siloed by team.

A named owner is accountable for data quality in the area this initiative would touch.

There's enough historical, labeled, or structured data to train or evaluate a model here.

Governance 04

A named executive is accountable for AI decisions and outcomes in this initiative.

There's a defined process for reviewing AI outputs for risk, compliance, or bias before they reach real decisions.

There's a way to monitor and course-correct the system after launch — not just sign-off at go-live.

Ecosystem 05 — Scored separately

AI tools or copilots are used in daily workflows by real teams — not a one-time pilot that quietly stopped.

More than one function or department already uses some form of AI — it isn't confined to a single isolated team.

There's an established vendor or platform stack for AI tools, rather than scattered one-off experiments per team.

Handoffs between AI output and human review are clearly defined — people know exactly when to trust it and when to check it.

Workflows were actually redesigned around AI, rather than AI bolted onto the same steps that existed before it.
